Integrated Behavioral Health in Oklahoma City

Research has shown that the mind and body are connected. When a person feels sad and blue, it can impact their energy level, sleep, and even how their body fights off illness. At Hands To Guide You, we believe that treating the whole person — not just symptoms — is the foundation of lasting wellness.

We work alongside your primary care physician to provide seamless, coordinated behavioral health services. Our integrated model means your mental and physical health are treated together, because there is no physical health without mental health.

From children and adolescents to adults and seniors, we serve patients at every stage of life with personalized, compassionate care rooted in evidence-based practices.

Why Us

What is Integrated Care and why is it important?

Integrated care is a model of healthcare delivery that brings together behavioral health (mental health and substance use) services with primary care and other physical health services, treating the whole person rather than just isolated symptoms. At Hands To Guide You, integrated care means your behavioral health provider communicates directly with your doctor, coordinates your treatment plan, and works as part of your complete healthcare team.

The evidence is clear: people with behavioral health conditions who receive integrated care experience better health outcomes, fewer hospitalizations, and a significantly higher quality of life. Research has demonstrated that integrated behavioral health treatment can reduce emergency department visits, lower rates of chronic disease complications, and dramatically improve day-to-day functioning — for patients of all ages and backgrounds.

Our multidisciplinary team at Hands To Guide You is specially trained in integrated care models. We use a combination of evidence-based therapies, advanced neurofeedback technology, and collaborative care coordination to address your needs in a way that no single-specialty provider can match. When you choose us, you're not just getting a counselor — you're gaining a whole-person care partner.

Neurofeedback & Brain Mapping Services

Neurofeedback at Hands To Guide You is a non-invasive, evidence-based treatment that uses real-time EEG feedback to help the brain learn healthier, more regulated patterns. Combined with QEEG brain mapping, we identify the exact brainwave irregularities contributing to your symptoms.

🧠 QEEG Brain Mapping & Neurofeedback

Non-invasive · Evidence-based · FDA-cleared QEEG · 20+ Years Experience

ADHD & Attention

Neurofeedback has been validated as a non-medication intervention for ADHD, helping the brain develop sustained focus, impulse control, and emotional regulation through targeted brainwave training.

PTSD & Trauma

By directly addressing the dysregulated brainwave patterns associated with trauma, neurofeedback helps reduce hyperarousal, flashbacks, and emotional reactivity without requiring verbal re-processing of traumatic events.

Anxiety Disorders

Neurofeedback trains the brain to produce calmer, more stable patterns, reducing the excessive beta activity that contributes to worry, panic, and generalized anxiety disorders.

Depression

Brain mapping identifies specific patterns of underactivation associated with depression. Neurofeedback training encourages healthier brainwave activity, often improving mood, motivation, and energy levels.

Sleep Disorders

Neurofeedback promotes healthy sleep architecture by training the brain to naturally shift into the calm, slow-wave states needed for restorative sleep — without medication dependence.

Chronic Stress

Prolonged stress dysregulates the nervous system. Neurofeedback provides targeted training to help the brain recover its natural resilience and self-regulation capacity.
