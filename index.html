About Our Founder

Dr. Larry Ford, DBH
Founder & Chief Executive

Dr. Ford founded Hands 2 Guide You in November 2011 on a singular conviction: there is no physical health without mental health. His integrated treatment model has reduced hospital readmissions by 30% in southwest Oklahoma City alone, earning national recognition and the prestigious Biodyne Life Change Award.

Board member of the Cummings Graduate Institute and widely regarded as an authority on integrated behavioral health in Oklahoma, Dr. Ford leads a compassionate, multidisciplinary team dedicated to whole-person wellness.
